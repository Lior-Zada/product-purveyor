## Why we picked this
- Lowest noise in the 45cm class at 42dB — material in our open-plan kitchen
- Steel interior + third tray, both hard requirements
- Bosch official import 3y warranty vs the cheaper Beko's 1y yevuan moy

## Alternatives considered
- **Beko BDIN16431** — ₪3,450, but 48dB and known for pump failures around year 5
- **Electra ESL45** — ₪2,900, IL warranty edge but only 9 place settings and basic feature set
- **Siemens SR256I00TE** — ₪4,200, near-identical to the Bosch but Bosch had better tashlumim count at Bug
- **Miele G 5050** — see upsell considered above
