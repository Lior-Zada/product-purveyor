# IL home-buying glossary

Quote one-line definitions inline the first time a term appears in a conversation. Don't re-define within the same session.

**Yevuan moy (יבוא מקביל)** — parallel import. The product is genuine but imported by a third party, not the official manufacturer rep. Cheaper, but warranty is shorter and serviced by the importer.

**Yevuan rishmi (יבוא רשמי) / official import** — imported by the manufacturer's authorized rep. Longer warranty (typically 3-5y vs 1-3y for yevuan moy), full manufacturer service network.

**Tashlumim (תשלומים)** — interest-free installments. Israeli credit cards routinely offer 12-36 tashlumim with no extra cost; many retailers compete on tashlumim count. Effectively a free loan.

**Hashmal (חשמל)** — electricity / electrical. When asked about hashmal, usually means: voltage (220V standard), main breaker amps, dedicated circuits, ground.

**Haranha (התקנה)** — installation. Many appliances quote price excluding haranha. Typical ranges: AC ₪800-2000, oven ₪200-400, washer ₪150, dishwasher ₪200-400 (incl. plumbing connection).

**Haavarat lakuach (העברת לקוח)** — used loosely for "consumer protection"; specifically refers to the 14-day return window for online purchases under Israeli law.

**Bituach lakuach (ביטוח לקוח)** — extended warranty / consumer insurance. Often pushed at point of sale; rarely worth it for appliances unless the manufacturer warranty is unusually short.

**Kosher / Sabbath mode (מצב שבת)** — disables auto-shutoff/lights/timers so observance is possible. Standard on most IL-imported appliances; often missing on personally-imported ones.

**Hard water (מים קשים)** — water with high mineral content. Causes scale buildup in kettles, dishwashers, washers, shower heads. Most of central Israel including Tel Aviv has moderate-hard water; the south is harder. Check `mei-{city}.co.il` for your area.

**COP (מקדם הספק)** — coefficient of performance, used for AC and heat pumps. Higher = more efficient. AC COP 4.0 = 4 units of cooling per unit of electricity. Israeli summers make this number really matter.

**SEER / EER** — seasonal/energy efficiency ratios. US-style equivalents to COP. Israeli labels usually use COP and energy class A-G.

**IPx rating** — water/dust resistance. IP44 = splash-resistant. IP68 = fully waterproof. Matters for outdoor lights and bathroom fixtures.

**Maaliyah (מעלית)** — elevator. Delivery services often charge per flight if there's no elevator; typical ₪50-150 per flight.

**Zap.co.il** — IL's largest price comparison site. Use as price floor reference.

**KSP, Bug, Ivory, Machsanei Hashmal** — major IL appliance retailers, generally trustworthy for warranty + service.

**Yad2 (יד 2)** — IL's biggest used-goods classifieds.

**Place settings** — dishwasher capacity unit. ~12 pieces per setting (plates + bowl + cup + utensils). 9 settings ≈ 1-2 person household; 13 ≈ 3-4; 14+ ≈ 5+.
